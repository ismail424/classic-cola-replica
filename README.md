## üß™ The Science of "The Real Thing"
To achieve the distinct "Commercial Taste" rather than a "Homebrew Taste," this protocol enforces three industrial standards:

1.  **Ethanol Extraction:** We do not use gums to bind oils. We use 95% Grain Alcohol to "cut" the oils, allowing them to dissolve into the syrup invisibly.
2.  **Sugar Inversion:** We heat the sucrose to $80^\circ C$ with acid. This splits the sugar molecules (inversion), preventing crystallization and mimicking the sweetness curve of High Fructose Corn Syrup (or the original cane sugar version).
3.  **Tannin Replication:** The original formula used "spent coca leaves" (leaves with cocaine removed). These leaves added bitterness and astringency (dryness). We replicate this strictly using **Wine Tannins** or specific tea extracts.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Manufacturing Instructions

### Phase 1: The "Cut" (Day 1)
1.  Combine all **Part A** oils into a glass beaker.
2.  Add the oils to the **Alcohol**. Stir vigorously.
3.  **Critical:** Let this mixture stand for **24 hours**. You will see a "flocculent" (cloudy separation) occur. This is the "terpenes" separating. This is normal and authentic to the 1886 process.

### Phase 2: The Syrup (Day 2)
1.  Dissolve **Sugar** in **Water** and heat to $80^\circ C$. Maintain for 15 minutes to invert the sugar.
2.  Add **Phosphoric Acid**. (Safety Warning: Wear gloves).
3.  Add **Caffeine** and **Tannins** while hot. Stir until dissolved.
4.  Allow syrup to cool to room temperature.

### Phase 3: Final Assembly
1.  Pour the **Alcohol/Oil Mix (Part A)** into the cooled **Syrup (Part B)**.
2.  Add **Caramel Color**, **Glycerin**, and **Vanilla**.
3.  Mix thoroughly.
